### Full Description
GlobalShop is the e-commerce platform of C&A Marketing Inc, a New Jersey corporation established in 2003 with over 20 years in retail and distribution.

The platform specializes in:
- AI and ChatGPT merchandise
- Consumer electronics and accessories
- Beauty and personal care products
- Home and lifestyle goods

Key features:
- Global warehouse network (US, Singapore, Germany)
- 3-7 day delivery to major markets
- Multi-tier seller program
- Secure payments via PayPal and Stripe

GlobalShop connects quality merchants with consumers worldwide, leveraging two decades of supply chain expertise.

# GlobalShop Seller Guide

Complete guide to selling on the GlobalShop platform.

## Getting Started

### Registration Process

1. **Create Account** - Visit [g-shops.net/seller/register](https://g-shops.net/seller/register)
2. **Submit Documents** - Business license, ID verification, bank details
3. **Review Period** - 1-3 business days for approval
4. **Store Setup** - Configure your storefront and branding
5. **List Products** - Upload inventory and set pricing
6. **Go Live** - Start receiving orders

### Account Requirements

| Seller Type | Requirements |
|-------------|--------------|
| Individual | Government ID, Phone, Email, Bank Account |
| Business | Business License, Tax ID, Company Bank Account |

## Seller Tiers

- T+15 settlement cycle

### Gold Seller ($999 one-time)

## Product Listing

### Supported Categories

| Category | Commission | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| Electronics | 8% | High demand |
| AI Products | 10% | Platform specialty |
| Beauty | 12% | Quality verification required |
| Home & Living | 8% | Size/weight limits apply |
| Fashion | 10% | Brand authorization needed |

### Listing Requirements

- **Images**: Minimum 800x800px, white background preferred
- **Title**: Clear, keyword-rich, under 200 characters
- **Description**: Detailed specs, materials, dimensions
- **Pricing**: Competitive with market rates
- **Inventory**: Accurate stock counts

### Prohibited Items

- Counterfeit products
- Weapons and ammunition
- Controlled substances
- Adult content
- Hazardous materials

## Fulfillment Options

### Merchant Fulfilled
Ship directly to customers using your preferred carriers.

- You handle packaging and shipping
- Upload tracking within 48 hours
- Customer service for shipping issues

### GlobalShop Warehouse (FBG)
Send inventory to our fulfillment centers.

| Warehouse | Location | Best For |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| US-East | New Jersey | North America |
| US-West | California | West Coast, Asia |
| SG | Singapore | Southeast Asia |
| EU | Germany | European Union |

**FBG Benefits:**
- Faster delivery times
- Prime-like badges
- Reduced shipping costs
- Returns handling

## Payments & Settlement

### Payment Schedule

| Tier | Settlement Cycle | Minimum Withdrawal |
|------|------------------|-------------------|
| Standard | T+15 | $100 |
| Verified | T+7 | $100 |
| Gold | T+3 | $50 |

### Withdrawal Methods

- PayPal Business
- Payoneer
- Bank Wire Transfer (Business accounts)
- Stripe Connect

### Fees

- Platform commission: 5-12% (tier-dependent)
- Payment processing: Handled by payment provider
- Withdrawal: Free from GlobalShop

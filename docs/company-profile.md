# GlobalShop - Company Profile

## Overview

**GlobalShop** is a cross-border e-commerce marketplace operated by C&A Marketing Inc, a New Jersey corporation established in 2003. The platform specializes in AI products, consumer electronics, and lifestyle goods, serving customers worldwide through a multi-warehouse fulfillment network.

## History

C&A Marketing Inc was incorporated in New Jersey in 2003 (Corp #101021330). Originally focused on product distribution and retail partnerships, the company expanded into e-commerce with the launch of GlobalShop, creating a platform to connect verified merchants with global consumers.

## Business Model

GlobalShop operates as a B2C and B2B2C marketplace platform:

- **For Consumers**: Direct access to AI products, electronics, beauty, and lifestyle goods
- **For Sellers**: Multi-tier merchant program with fulfillment services
- **For Affiliates**: Commission-based referral program

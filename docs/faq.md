# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is GlobalShop?
GlobalShop (GlobalShop) is a cross-border e-commerce platform specializing in AI products, electronics, and lifestyle goods. We connect global buyers with verified merchants through our multi-warehouse fulfillment network.

### Where is GlobalShop based?
Our headquarters is in Edison, New Jersey, USA. We operate fulfillment centers in the United States, Singapore, and Germany.

### What makes GlobalShop different?
- Specialized focus on AI/ChatGPT merchandise
- Global warehouse network for fast delivery
- Verified seller program for quality assurance
- Competitive pricing with buyer protection

## For Buyers

### How long does shipping take?
| Your Location | Warehouse Used | Delivery Time |
|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| USA | US Warehouse | 3-7 business days |
| Europe | Germany Warehouse | 3-7 business days |
| Southeast Asia | Singapore Warehouse | 2-5 business days |
| Other regions | Direct shipping | 7-21 business days |

### What payment methods do you accept?
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay / Google Pay
- Klarna (select regions)

### What is your return policy?
- **7-day no-reason returns** for most items
- **15-day quality guarantee** for defective products
- Some categories excluded (perishables, intimate items, custom orders)
- Return shipping paid by party at fault

### How do I track my order?
Log into your account and visit "My Orders" for real-time tracking. You'll also receive email updates at each shipping milestone.

### Is my payment secure?
Yes. We use SSL encryption and are PCI DSS compliant. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal).

## For Sellers

### How do I become a seller?
1. Register at g-shops.net/seller/register
2. Submit required documents
3. Wait for approval (1-3 business days)
4. Set up your store and list products
5. Start selling!

### What documents do I need?
- **Individuals**: Government ID, phone, email, bank account
- **Businesses**: Business license, tax ID, company bank account

### What are the fees?
| Tier | Upfront Fee | Commission | Settlement |
|------|-------------|------------|------------|
| Standard | Free | 5% | T+15 |
| Verified | $299 | 8% | T+7 |
| Gold | $999 | 12% | T+3 |

### How do I get paid?
Withdrawals available via PayPal, Payoneer, or bank transfer. Minimum withdrawal is $100 ($50 for Gold sellers). Processing takes 1-3 business days.

### Can I use GlobalShop warehouses?
Yes! Our Fulfillment by GlobalShop (FBG) program lets you store inventory in our US, Singapore, and EU warehouses for faster customer delivery.

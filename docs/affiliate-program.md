# GlobalShop Affiliate Program

Earn commissions by promoting GlobalShop products to your audience.

## Program Overview

The GlobalShop Affiliate Program allows content creators, bloggers, influencers, and website owners to earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through their referral links.

### Key Benefits

- **Competitive Rates**: Up to 15% commission per sale
- **30-Day Cookies**: Extended attribution window
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Monitor clicks, conversions, earnings
- **Monthly Payouts**: Reliable payment schedule
- **Marketing Assets**: Banners, links, product feeds

## How It Works

### 1. Sign Up
Register at [g-shops.net/affiliates](https://g-shops.net/affiliates)

**Requirements:**
- Active website, blog, or social media presence
- Minimum 1,000 followers/monthly visitors
- Content relevant to our product categories
- No adult, violent, or illegal content

### 2. Get Approved
Review takes 2-5 business days. You'll receive:
- Unique affiliate ID
- Dashboard access
- API credentials (optional)

### 3. Promote Products
- Generate custom links for any product
- Use provided banners and widgets
- Create content featuring GlobalShop products
- Share on social media

### 4. Earn Commissions
- 30-day cookie attribution
- Commission on full cart value
- Real-time tracking in dashboard

### 5. Get Paid
- Monthly payouts (15th of each month)
- Minimum threshold: $50
- Payment via PayPal or bank transfer

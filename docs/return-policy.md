# Return & Refund Policy

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026

C&A Marketing Inc, operating as GlobalShop, wants you to be satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines our return and refund procedures.

## Return Eligibility

### Standard Returns (7 Days)
Most items can be returned within **7 days** of delivery for any reason.

**Conditions:**
- Item unused and in original condition
- Original packaging intact
- All accessories and tags included
- Proof of purchase required

### Quality Issues (15 Days)
Defective or damaged items can be returned within **15 days** of delivery.

**Covered:**
- Manufacturing defects
- Damage during shipping
- Wrong item received
- Missing components

## Non-Returnable Items

The following categories cannot be returned unless defective:

- Perishable goods
- Intimate apparel and swimwear
- Personalized/custom items
- Downloadable software
- Gift cards
- Hazardous materials
- Items marked "Final Sale"

## Return Process

### Step 1: Request Return
- Log into your GlobalShop account
- Go to "My Orders"
- Select order and click "Return Item"
- Choose reason and submit

### Step 2: Approval
- Review within 24-48 hours
- Return shipping label provided (if applicable)
- Return authorization number issued

### Step 3: Ship Item
- Pack securely in original packaging
- Attach provided shipping label
- Drop off at designated carrier
- Keep tracking number

### Step 4: Refund
- Inspection upon receipt (1-3 business days)
- Refund processed to original payment method
- 3-5 business days for funds to appear

## Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for:
- Items not in original condition
- Missing components or packaging
- Items returned after 7 days (case-by-case)

## Exchanges

We currently process returns and new orders separately.

**For exchanges:**
1. Return original item for refund
2. Place new order for replacement

## International Returns

International customers are responsible for:
- Return shipping costs
- Customs duties on returned items
- Using trackable shipping method

## Seller-Fulfilled Orders

For items shipped directly by third-party sellers:
- Return policies may vary
- Contact seller through order page
- GlobalShop facilitates disputes if needed

## Damaged or Lost Returns

- Use trackable shipping
- Keep shipping receipt
- Report issues within 7 days of shipping
- GlobalShop not responsible for lost returns without tracking

## Cancellations

### Before Shipping
- Full refund, no fees
- Cancel through "My Orders"

### After Shipping
- Refuse delivery, or
- Return per standard policy
